Our Story

  • First We Meet

    December 13TH, 2013

    Despite working at the same company for over a month, and admiring each other from afar, our first conversation took place at the company Christmas party. After hearing Richard's infamous "Poo Story" (yes, he really did crack that out on our first meeting), Cassie was sold, and they planned their first date for a few days later.

  • First Date

    December 19th, 2013

    After trying (and failing) to both sneakily leave the office at the same and meet outside without our colleagues seeing, we had our first date at The Royal Oak pub in Clapham Common. Numerous drinks later, Richard named our newly formed table tennis team "Team Casstaff", a nickname which has since stuck.

  • The Proposal

    June 1st, 2017

    On our first night of what turned out to be the most perfect holiday in Dubrovnik, Croatia, Richard got down on one knee and proposed on the beach at sunset. It was just us, and a little old Croatian fisherwoman on the next bay across, and it was perfect.
